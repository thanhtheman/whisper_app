Why do we build
Whisper?

Building a new habit is all about daily repetition. Daily repetition needs reminders and inspirations.

The problem is the world is a noisy place with distractions such as social media. This makes a simple action of opening your notebook or laptop to review your commitment is extremely difficult. That's how you lose track of your commitment.

The solution is a simple text message that reminds you exactly what you need to do everyday.

How to get
started?

Start small and start NOW.

Sign up Whisper for free, creating your goals or an inspiring quote/video that prompt you take actions.

Set specific time that you want Whisper to send you those text messages. The first 30 messages are free!
